Main Services

Textile Waste Collection and Sorting

This service involves collaborating with local textile facilities to collect pre-industrial and post-consumer textile waste. The waste is sorted for recycling, ensuring a steady supply of materials for garment production.
more
Recycling and Garment Production

Through innovative recycling processes, textile waste is transformed into high-quality garments. This process reduces waste and promotes a circular economy in the textile industry.
more
Training and Capacity Building

A "train-the-trainer" program is implemented to educate industry workers and community members on sustainable textile recycling techniques. This builds local capacity in circular economy practices.
more
Sustainable Supply Chain Management

M.J EcoSpin Innovations integrates waste collection, processing, manufacturing, and quality control into a streamlined, eco-friendly supply chain. This ensures efficiency and minimizes the environmental impact.
more
Community Engagement & Awareness

The project organizes engagement sessions to raise awareness about the environmental benefits of recycling and sustainability in the textile industry. These sessions encourage local adoption of eco-friendly practices.
more
Monitoring and Evaluation

A progress tracking system monitors the project's outcomes, such as the quantity of waste diverted and garments produced. This ensures accountability and helps measure the project's environmental and economic impact.
more
